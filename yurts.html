YURTS

The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxurious yurts offer a perfect blend of comfort and nature. Raised four feet off the ground, they provide stunning views and excellent ventilation. Each yurt features weather-resistant canvas walls, a cozy timber floor, and a retractable dome for stargazing. With private decks for relaxation and total seclusion, these yurts deliver an exceptional glamping experience in a serene and picturesque setting.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a gas fireplace, king-size bed with a down quilt, comfy couch, dining table, mini-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker. It also includes a sink with hot and cold running water for added convenience. Shower and restroom facilities are available in the lodge. This luxurious combination of comfort and nature offers a unique, serene glamping experience.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and dress in light layers to adapt to changing weather conditions. A flashlight is also helpful for evening walks or low-light situations. Being prepared for the outdoors ensures a more enjoyable experience. Above all, guests bring a sense of adventure, ready to explore, hike, or simply relax in the natural beauty surrounding them, making for a memorable stay.
